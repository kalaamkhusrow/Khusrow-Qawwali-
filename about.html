About Khanquah Amjadia

Haque Haque Haque – Aastana Aliya Hazrat Syed Shah Amjad Ali Sultan Rangila Chishti Sabri (Alayhir Rahma) aur Hazrat Syed Shah Tasadduq Ali Sultan Asad Chishti Sabri Amjadi (Alayhir Rahma) ka paak o muqaddas silsila, Khanquah Amjadia, Station Road, Siwan, Bihar mein waqeh hai.

Ye roohani markaz sirf ek Khanquah nahi, balke ek noorani chashma hai jahan se logon ke dilon ko roohani sukoon, ishq-e-ilahi aur barkat milti hai. Hazrat Amjad Ali Sultan Rangila (RA) ne apni poori zindagi insaniyat ki khidmat, sulh-o-aman aur tasawwuf ke paigham ke liye waqf kar di.

Aap ka silsila Hazrat Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti Ajmeri (RA) se chalta hua Hazrat Khwaja Nizamuddin Auliya (RA), Hazrat Ameer Khusrow (RA), aur Shah Sabir Kalyari (RA) tak ja milta hai. Aap ki mehfil-e-sama, Qawwali, aur zikr-e-ilahi ki riwayat aaj bhi logon ke dilon ko roshan karti hai.

Aapke baad is silsile ko mazid roohani balandi mili Hazrat Syed Shah Tasadduq Ali Sultan Asad Chishti Sabri Amjadi (RA) ke zariye, jinhone awaam mein roohani ilm aur sukoon pohnchaya, aur Khanquahi riwayat ko mazbooti se zinda rakha.

Aaj bhi ye silsila roshan hai aur roshni bant raha hai Peerzadah Hazrat Dr. Syed Shah Iltefat Ahmad Amjadi Madazillahu Aali aur Peerzadah Hazrat Syed Shah Tanweer Ahmad Amjadi (Maujood Chishti) ke zariye. Aap dono buzurg Khanquah Amjadia ki qiyaadat farma rahe hain aur roohaniyat ka paigham har dil tak pohncha rahe hain.

Ye Khanquah ek markaz hai:
– Ishq-e-Elahi ka
– Roohani Taleem aur Dars-o-Naseehat ka
– Qawwali, Sama aur Zikr-e-Qalb ka
– Sulh, Aman aur Barkat ka

Yahan har saal Urs Shareef, Mehfil-e-Sama, Taleemati silsile, aur Khidmat-e-Khalq ke kai program hotay hain jismein hazarhon log faizan lete hain.
